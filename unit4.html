About us




Our bakery is more than just a business , it's a labour of love .
We are a family owned and operated bakery that has been serving our community for years.
Our story began with a simple passion for baking and a dream to share that passion with our community.


Over the years we've perfected our recipes , experimented with new flavours and built a reputation for quality and excellence.
Today we're proud to be part of so many special moments from birthday to weddings , and from everyday treats to holiday traditions ,
because our goal is to provide you with the best possible baking experience everytime .










Our story



It all started in Kimberly’s cozy kitchen, where late-night baking experiments turned into mouthwatering masterpieces.
What began as a hobby — just a girl, her mixer, and a serious sweet tooth — quickly became a local legend. Friends couldn't stop raving.
Family kept asking for more. And before she knew it, Kimberly was baking her way into hearts all over town.
Today, Kimberly’s Sweet Treats is all about spreading joy, one cookie at a time.
We're still baking with that same homemade love — just with a little more cookie dough and a whole lot more sprinkles!
